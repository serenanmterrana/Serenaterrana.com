School Crusades

From early morning until dinner time, we went school to school preaching the glorious Gospel. We witnessed many decisions for Christ and saw the Holy Spirit move powerfully in the lives of the students.

Indigenous

We traveled up a high mountain to reach a group of people who had never heard the Gospel before. We brought clothes and treats for the local community. This group speaks Q’iche, so we had to interpret the Gospel across three languages as we shared the message with them.
